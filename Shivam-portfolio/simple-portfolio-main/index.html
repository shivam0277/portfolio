Monument detection using Deep learning

"Monument Detection using Deep Learning" is a cutting-edge project that harnesses the capabilities of deep learning techniques to automatically identify and recognize landmarks and monuments. By training a neural network on vast datasets of images, this project empowers computers to distinguish and categorize famous architectural and historical landmarks. Whether it's iconic buildings, statues, or natural wonders, this technology can revolutionize tourism, geolocation services, and cultural preservation efforts. With this project, we're paving the way for efficient monument recognition and enhanced user experiences in various applications, including travel and augmented reality.
